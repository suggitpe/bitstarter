Family Ties

Organising a family is hard, making sure they all know what's going on is even harder.

I come from a big family which involves lots of planning. Life is hectic which means we often forget to tell each other things: the simple things and and the important things. Trying to keep everyone up to date is like herding cats.

Family Ties aims to help this by bringing the family together using mobile and internet technologies.

About

Social media is, well to be honest, too social. What a family needs is private media that they can all share, knowing that it will always remain private to them:

  • Calendar / planner for whats coming up
    • Doctors appointments
    • Weddings
  • Weekly menus based on family favourite menus/recipes
  • Shopping lists
  • Family chats / messages
  • To-do lists for the weekend jobs
  • Upload family photos
  • Online storage for family archives
  • Locate family member (location services)
  • Extend the family with relatives that have limited access

Information about your family needs to be available to all the family, all of the time. Whether you are out and about or away from each other, having your family close by is important to you.

FAQ

Why is this different to the other social media offerings?
In short privacy. Other social media offerings work on the principle that the data is essentially public. In our offering we work on the principle that all data is private unless you chose to share it.
Will I have to pay for the service?
You can chose to pay nothing and use the bare-bones services including all the mobile phone applications. Should you wish to you can opt in for value add services such as:
  • Advert free mobile phone applications
  • Advert free web application
  • Data storage above 1Gb
  • Extended family above 10 people
  • Location services
